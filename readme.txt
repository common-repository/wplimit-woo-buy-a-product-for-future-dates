== Description ==
WPLimit Woo Buy a Product for Future Dates is a plugin where you can Buy a product for future dates. It's simple to use for anyone. You can use this plugin for many purpose such as:
1. Buying Events tickets for future dates.
2. Ordering lunch for School in future dates.
3. Ordering Food from a restaurants for future dates.
4. Fixing a future dates for meating.
5. and many more.....

[Live Demo Product](https://wplimit.com/woo-buy-a-product-for-future-dates/product/beanie/) | [Documentation](https://wplimit.com/documentation/) | [Contact](https://wplimit.com/contact/)



== Installation ==
* Before activate this plugin. You must remember to activate the woocomerce plugin. Because you know this is woocommerce based plugin.
1. Upload the entire WPLimit Woo Buy a Product for Future Dates` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

After installation, the plugin will add a tab under the product called "WPLimit Future Dates".

For more information have a look at the [Online Documentation](https://wplimit.com/documentation/).
